About /middle/

/middle/

Welcome to /middle/! Need to meet up with a group of disparate people? This web application collects geocodes from multiple users and finds their geographic center. This way you can make the ultimate compromise in finding restaurants or venues to meet at.

Getting Started

When you first open the application, it opens the Group List screen. This screen shows you any past searches that you've initiated or been invited to. From here, you can create a new Group, or reminisce about earlier ones.

Creating a Group

Each Group requires a name. This makes it easier to quickly identify Groups at a glance. The Group name shows up in the Group List and Group Details screens. Once you've decided on your creative name, click the Go! button to enter the Group Details screen.

Group Details

Here's where the magic begins! Copy the link at the top of the screen and send it to your friends! As more people join the Group, their locations get added to the map, and the geographic center is calculated. You can change the algorithm used to determine the center, and you can toggle people on or off the map.
